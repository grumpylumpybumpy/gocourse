Logical Operators: 

    ! (Logical NOT)
    || (logcical OR) a > b || a > c
    && (logical AND) a > b && c > d

    Bitwise Operators:

    & (bitwise AND)
    | (bitwise OR)
    ^ (bitwise XOR) exclusive OR
    &^ (bitwise, AND NOT)
    << (left shift)
    >> (right shift)

    Comparison Operators:

    == (equal)
    != (not equal)
    < (less than)
    <= (less than or equal to)
    > (greater than)
    >= (greater than or equal to)
